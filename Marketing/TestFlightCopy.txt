1) APP STORE CONNECT — APP PRIVACY → PRIVACY POLICY (TEXT FIELD)
================================================================================

Sodalite does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The
bundled Apple Privacy Manifest declares zero data collection.

What the app stores on your device:
- Server credentials (Jellyfin/Seerr URL, access token, session cookie,
  optionally your password) in the system Keychain. Used to keep you
  signed in across restarts.
- Playback preferences (preferred audio language, autoplay, etc.) in
  UserDefaults.
- Image cache (posters, backdrops downloaded from your Jellyfin server)
  in the app's local cache directory. Cleared by the system when storage
  is low or when you uninstall the app.

What the app sends over the network:
- HTTPS/HTTP requests only to the Jellyfin and (optional) Seerr servers
  you choose: login, library listings, playback info, media segments,
  session reports, image downloads.
- No requests to the developer or any third party. No analytics
  endpoints, no crash-reporting SDKs, no advertising networks, no
  telemetry of any kind.

Third-party services:
- None. Sodalite uses no third-party SDKs for analytics, crash
  reporting, advertising, or tracking.
- The Jellyfin and Seerr servers you connect to are operated by you or
  by whoever you choose to trust; they are outside the scope of this
  policy.

Children:
- Sodalite collects no personal data from anyone, including children.

Changes:
- If this policy ever changes, the updated version will appear at
  https://sodalite.superuser404.de/privacy and the App Store listing
  will reference the new revision date.

Controller / Contact:
- Vincent Herbst, contactable via the Imprint at
  https://sodalite.superuser404.de/imprint or by opening an issue at
  https://github.com/superuser404notfound/Sodalite/issues

Full bilingual policy (English + German, GDPR-aligned):
https://sodalite.superuser404.de/privacy

3) TESTFLIGHT — BETA APP DESCRIPTION
================================================================================

Sodalite is a fast, native Apple TV client for Jellyfin with built-in
Jellyseerr integration. Real HDR10, Dolby Vision, and Dolby Atmos
passthrough (EAC3+JOC wrapped as Dolby MAT 2.0). Apple TV Top Shelf
integration, Siri "Continue Watching" voice command, 26 languages.

Requirements: Apple TV 4K, tvOS 26 or later, your own Jellyfin server
(10.9+ recommended). Optional: Jellyseerr (2.0+) for the request flow.

Open source under GPL-3.0 (App Store Exception) at
https://github.com/superuser404notfound/Sodalite

Privacy: Sodalite does not collect, transmit, or share any personal
data. Communication is only with your own Jellyfin/Seerr servers — no
analytics, no third-party SDKs, no telemetry. The bundled Privacy
Manifest confirms this. Full policy:
https://sodalite.superuser404.de/privacy

4) TESTFLIGHT — WHAT TO TEST
================================================================================

Focus areas where outside feedback is most valuable:

- HDR / Dolby Vision display switching across different TV models and
  the Match Content setting
- Atmos passthrough on various AVRs (your receiver might do something
  mine doesn't)
- Server discovery on weird network setups (mDNS off, multiple subnets,
  VPN, IPv6-only)
- Jellyseerr request flow when your library partially overlaps the
  Seerr catalog
- Top Shelf cells: do they update reliably after watching something?
- Siri "Continue Watching" voice command on different Siri Remote
  generations
- Anything that crashes

Bug reports: TestFlight feedback (screenshot + system info), or GitHub
Issues at https://github.com/superuser404notfound/Sodalite/issues
